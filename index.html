Car repair and maintenance services

Services We Offer

VULCANIZATION

Our vulcanization service repairs small tire punctures by applying a heat-activated adhesive patch that restores the tire’s integrity quickly and reliably. This efficient method keeps your tires roadworthy without the need for immediate replacement.

ENGINE OIL AND FILTERS REPLACEMENT

We offer a rapid engine oil and filters replacement service that removes old oil and debris before refilling with premium oil and a new filter. This routine maintenance helps optimize engine performance and prolongs its lifespan.

AIR CONDITIONING RECHARGE AND MAINTENANCE

Our air conditioning recharge and maintenance service swiftly diagnoses your AC system, removes moisture, and replenishes refrigerant to restore peak cooling performance. It’s designed to keep you comfortable during hot weather with minimal downtime.
